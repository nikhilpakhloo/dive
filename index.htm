ABOUT US
Artisan & Ocean was founded in 2013 by a team of experienced divers on a mission to make SCUBA diving safe, easy and fun with its compact and affordable diving gear device, DIVEROID. DIVEROID provides critical safety information for diving. This all-in-one solution measures not only depth, dive duration and NDL to prevent accidents and decompression sickness, but also offers underwater camera, compass and auto-synced logbook functions.

Experience the dramatic moment with DIVEROID.
